Okpa Trivia

When was Okpa first sited in Nigeria?

Jeffery M. Pilcher, Okpa Historian and a Proffessor of History at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of Okpa in Nigeria are in the Newpaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine)
